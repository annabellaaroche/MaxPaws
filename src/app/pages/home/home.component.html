Shiba Inu Dog Breed

The Shiba Inu is the smallest of the six original and distinct spitz breeds of dog from Japan. A small, agile dog that copes very well with mountainous terrain, the Shiba Inu was originally bred for hunting.

Golden Retriever Dog Breed

The Golden Retriever is a friendly, intelligent, and devoted dog breed. They are well-known for their golden coat and their friendly nature, making them great family pets and companions.

Bulldog Dog Breed

Bulldogs are known for their loose, wrinkled skin and distinctive pushed-in nose. They are gentle and friendly dogs, making them great companions for families.
